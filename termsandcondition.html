We value and prioritize the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how it is used, and the measures we take to protect your information. By using our Blood Donation System, you consent to the collection and use of your personal information as described in this policy.

1. Information Collection and Use
1.1 Personal Information:
When you register for our Blood Donation System, we may collect personal information such as your name, contact information (email address, phone number), age, blood type, and any other relevant information necessary for the blood donation process.
1.2 Usage Data: We may also collect non-personal information about your usage of the Blood Donation System, such as your IP address, browser type, device information, and operating system. This information is used to analyze trends, administer the system, and improve our services.

2. Information Usage
2.1 Blood Donation Process:
The personal information collected is used to facilitate the blood donation process. It may be shared with authorized healthcare providers or blood banks to ensure safe and effective blood transfusions.
2.2 Communication:
We may use your contact information to communicate with you about your blood donation appointments, updates, and relevant information related to the Blood Donation System.
2.3 System Improvement:
We may analyze the non-personal information collected to improve the functionality, performance, and user experience of the Blood Donation System.

3. Information Sharing
3.1 Authorized Parties:
We may share your personal information with authorized healthcare providers, blood banks, or other entities involved in the blood donation process, to ensure the safe and effective handling of blood transfusions.
3.2 Legal Requirements:
We may disclose your personal information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal request.

4. Data Security
4.1 Protection Measures:
We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include encryption, secure data storage, and restricted access to personal information.
4.2 User Responsibility:
While we strive to protect your personal information, it is essential that you also take precautions to ensure the security of your account. Please keep your login credentials confidential and notify us immediately if you suspect any unauthorized access or activity.

5. Data Retention
We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, or as required by applicable laws and regulations.

6. Your Rights
You have the right to access, update, and correct your personal information in our records. If you wish to exercise these rights or have any questions regarding your personal information, please contact us using the information provided below.

7. Changes to the Privacy Policy
We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last Updated" date will be revised accordingly. Please review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we handle and protect your personal information.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies
8.1 Cookies:
We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on the Blood Donation System. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us recognize you and provide personalized features. You have the option to accept or decline cookies. However, please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality and user experience of the system.
8.2 Analytics:
We may use third-party analytics tools to gather information about your usage of the Blood Donation System. 14.1 Right to Access:
You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you. Upon receiving such a request, we will provide you with a copy of the information within a reasonable timeframe.
